Procedures at Time of Death

1. Contact

Funeral Director contacts Eternal Educational Gift Donation (EEGD) directly after death at (231) 620-3658 to determine next procedure.

2. Counsel

Funeral Director counsels donor’s family or legal representative that acceptance of donation is not guaranteed and requires approval by EEGD.

3. Form Completion

Donor’s family or legal representative completes the Medical Information Form and the Notification or Authorization by Donor Representative Form. Forms may be returned to EEGD via fax or e-mail to the following: (231) 938‑2248/info@eegdonation.com

4. Accept or Decline

EEGD determines whether donation is accepted or declined. If declined, decedent’s family or legal representative arranges for final disposition at own expense.

5. If Accepted:

Funeral Director arranges for transportation of donor’s body to the EEGD and provides the following documents to EEGD:
