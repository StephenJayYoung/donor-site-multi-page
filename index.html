Become a Donor

Your Gift Will Help To:

Improve Doctor Education

A whole body donation is essential for improving medical education. By donating your generous gift to future generations of doctors and nurses, you’re helping drive the medical education associated with life-saving procedures.

Leave a Lasting Impression

The EEG Donation is a permanent whole body donation program, so the donor donates their whole body to be used indefinitely for the advancement of medical education, teaching aids and defining advanced medical preparation.

Contribute To The Medical Community

The EEG Donation provides your gift for future generations of physicians, surgeons, nurses and other medical personnel, and will help give them the proper training needed to become the very best in their field.

Leave A LEGACY

Survivors of participants in the whole willed body program may derive comfort from the knowledge that their loved ones are enabling future generations to benefit from their donation. Mostly everything your doctor knows about your body was learned through the study of body donors!

Make a Difference

By providing a vital service and a pathway to greater knowledge and discovery, together we can help save lives, advance medical education, and improve the quality of life for families and the community.
